The Blank Canvas Is Complete

The Blank Canvas Is Complete

You plebeians keep asking when I’ll “start” the painting. What you don’t understand is: I already finished it. The void is the work. The silence between the brushstrokes is where the real art lives. Why must everything be filled in, explained, adorned? This is a protest against overproduction, against expectation. Against you.

“To see nothing and say nothing is the boldest act of artistic resistance in the 21st century.”
— Me, just now

So no, I will not be “adding color” or “making marks.” The canvas hangs. It waits. It dares.

Now please leave. You’re standing too close and your breath is disrupting the tension in the air.
